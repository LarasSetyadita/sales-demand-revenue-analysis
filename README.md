# 📊 Sales Data Analysis for Business Strategy

## 📌 Overview
This project focuses on analyzing sales data to generate actionable business insights that support strategic decision-making. The analysis aims to identify product performance, regional demand, sales trends, and revenue contribution to help optimize business operations.

## 🎯 Business Objectives
The analysis is designed to answer key business questions:

1. What are the top 5 best-selling products?
2. Which regions have the highest demand?
3. How does sales performance change over time?
4. What is the revenue contribution of each product category?
5. Which regions generate the highest revenue?

## 🧠 Key Insights
#### 🔝 Top-Selling Products
- Electric Kettle (146 units)
- Air Purifier (139 units)
- Blender Cleaner (114 units)
- Vacuum Cleaner (112 units)
- Rice Cooker (101 units)

👉 Insight: High demand products indicate strong market preference.

## 🌍 Regional Demand
- Highest demand: Jakarta & Surabaya (140 units each)
- Followed by: Makassar, Medan, Bandung

👉 Insight: Certain regions show stronger purchasing power and demand concentration.

## 📉 Sales Trends
- Significant drop in February
- Increase again in March
- Low performance in May–June, except Electric Kettle

👉 Insight: Seasonal patterns affect sales performance.

## 📊 Revenue Contribution
- All product categories contribute relatively evenly
- Contribution range: 15.49% – 23.85%

👉 Insight: No single product dominates revenue → balanced portfolio

## 💰 Highest Revenue Regions
Surabaya (highest)
Followed by: Jakarta, Medan, Makassar, Bandung

👉 Insight: Certain cities act as key revenue drivers

## 🚀 Business Recommendations
- Increase production for high-demand products (Electric Kettle & Air Purifier)
- Optimize inventory distribution in high-demand regions (Jakarta & Surabaya)
- Apply targeted promotions in low-performing regions
- Use product bundling strategy to boost low-demand items
- Investigate causes of sales decline in February
- Implement seasonal marketing campaigns based on trends
- Focus on high-revenue regions with loyalty programs and promotions
